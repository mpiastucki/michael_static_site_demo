What is Woven City?

Woven City is a test course for mobility to realize our dream of creating well-being for all. Starting from Susono by Mt. Fuji, we will conduct trials of new ideas for systems and services that expand mobility and unlock human potential. Here we will build the future fabric of life.

Where is Woven City?

Woven City is in Shizuoka prefecture, in Japan!
